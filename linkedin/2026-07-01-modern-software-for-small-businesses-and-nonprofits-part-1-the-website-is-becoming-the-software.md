The next generation of small-business and nonprofit websites will not just describe the organization. They will help the organization do the work.

This starts a new series on modern software for small businesses and nonprofits.

AI agents and inexpensive hosted services are changing the old boundary between a static website and a dynamic software project. A welding shop can collect better quote details. A nonprofit can coordinate volunteer handoffs. A food pantry can turn shelf updates into a practical grocery list.

The point is not that every small organization should custom-build everything. The point is that the first useful custom workflow is becoming much more affordable.
