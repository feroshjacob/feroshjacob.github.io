Amy Givens had owned the MyAmy Designs domain since high school.

For years, it represented one dream. After more than two decades in residential construction, remodeling, purchasing, estimating, project management, and warranty, it became the digital front door for MyAmy Designs & Remodeling.

This article is not about AI replacing experience. It is about an experienced construction professional using ChatGPT and Codex as tools to turn a long-held business vision into a working website.

The technology accelerated the process. Amy's expertise, judgment, taste, and persistence made the business real.
